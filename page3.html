THYME by Nicole A grassroots business

THYME: Feeding Tel Aviv's young and lazy

Nicole loves to cook. It's really as simple as that. She's used to hosting 10 people for a 4-course dinner... and loves every minute of it.

Something about preparing delicious homemade meals and seeing satisfied faces (and bellies) makes Nicole extremley happy.
When one of her dinner guests suggested that she prepare his meals every night of the week, Nicole realized she had a business - and THYME by Nicole was born.

Quality of ingredients, and tastes that remind you of home, are what makes THYME tick.
There are a variety of meals (examples below), each carefully selected to ensure you are eating healthy, balanced meals - every night of the week.

  • Roasted Garlic Chicken, Potatoes, & Green Salad
  • Lasagna & Caesar Salad
  • Parmesan Crusted White Fish, Quinioa, & Green Beans
  • Fresh Spring Rolls with Grilled Tofu and Fresh Vegetables with Peanut Dipping Sauce & Roasted Sweet Potato
  • Sweet and Spicy Chicken Wings, Couscous, & Roasted Cauliflower
